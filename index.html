The government and corporations have no right to be spying on our most private moments. #IFEELNAKED is a movement of posting your most private moments online to show Congress they should be ashamed that they’re collecting them. On a day soon, a coalition of websites will block all access to members of Congress, showing just these photos instead. Join the protest.

How to Participate

Step 1: Post a personal photo (anything Congress should be ashamed of spying on) to Twitter or Instagram.

Step 2: Be sure to include the #IFEELNAKED hashtag and a link back to this site.
Here's an example post, but feel free to be creative!

Dear Congress: do you and the NSA get off looking at our private photos? #ifeelnaked ifeelnaked.com

For Website owners:

If you have a website, blog or tumblr, you can join in the Congress Blackout. On May 15, two weeks before the PATRIOT Act is up for renewal, anyone browsing your site from Congress or the NSA will be redirected to our photos. To participate, just embed this code before the closing </BODY> tag on your page:

<script src="https://fightforthefuture.github.io/ifeelnaked.js/ifeelnaked.js"></script>
